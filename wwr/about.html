Family Fun

At Club Rafting we pride ourselves in getting families together to spend quality time and fun in the sun. Mom, Dad and the kids and all enjoy an adventure like no other.

Team Work

Bring your colleagues from work for a different kind of team building. Ride the rapids together, paddle as a team and spend quality time outdoors under the stars, around a roaring camp fire.

Adrenaline Rush

Does your daily routine need an injection of excitement? Do you need to feel alive? How about something to talk about at the office Christmas Party! Join us and make new memories and experiences to share with those around you.

HISTORY

Club Rafting have been running the rapids since 2005. We started off as a small team of two guides on the Tugela River, offering two and five-day packages for adrenaline junkies. Since then we have come to know from experience all that the mighty Tugela River has to offer. As a result we now offer two, five and ten day tours as well as day trips for day visitors.
