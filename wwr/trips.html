CLUB RAFTING

Twin Rapids Class II

This is our beginners tour or for those less adventurous. It is also our shortest river run for the day trippers. The rapids are easily negotiable and the whole family can join in, from junior to granny and grandpa. The total river time is approximately is about two hours. There is a stop midway for something light to eat and drink. Adventurers will have an opportunity to swim in the river and enjoy some time in the sun before continuing to the end.

Devils Cauldron Class III

This river course will need to be run wit a bit of courage. There are rapids with high, irregular waves and narrow passages that often require precise manouvering.

Whitewater Madness Class IV

Long, difficult rapids with constricted passages that often require complex manouvering in turbulent water. The course may be hard to detrmine, and scouting is often necessary

HISTORY

Club Rafting have been running the rapids since 2005. We started off as a small team of two guides on the Tugela River, offering two and five-day packages for adrenaline junkies. Since then we have come to know from experience all that the mighty Tugela River has to offer. As a result we now offer two, five and ten day tours as well as day trips for day visitors.
